The film was based on [[Eduard Uspensky]]'s stories about the characters.\n\nThis film was popular in the Soviet Union and spawned three sequels: ''[[Cheburashka (film)|Cheburashka]]'' (1972), ''[[Shapoklyak (film)|Shapoklyak]]'' (1974) and ''[[Cheburashka Goes to School]]'' (1983).\n\n==Plot summary==\n[[Gena the Crocodile]] works as a zoo animal at an urban zoo. Every evening, he returns home to his lonely apartment. Gena gets very tired of playing chess against himself and decides to find some friends to play with. Animals and people respond to advertisements that he posts all around the city. First, a girl named Galya comes with a homeless puppy, who is then followed by [[Cheburashka]].
