The film stars [[Jack Plotnick]] and premiered at the [[2012 Sundance Film Festival]]. It was part of the [[Toronto International Film Festival]]'s Official Selection. The producers of the film are Gregory Bernard, Charles-Marie Anthonioz, Nicolas Lhermitte and Josef Lieck.\n\n==Synopsis==\nDolph wakes up early in the morning in his [[suburb]]an home to find that his [[dog]] is missing. He is in [[denial]] that he has lost his job and shows up to his office to work every day and pretends to do his work. Dolph's need to live in a world of denial about all the areas in his life that are obviously not working is found throughout his life. A [[Arecaceae|palm tree]] in his backyard transforms into a [[pine|pine tree]]. It's raining inside Dolph's office even though none of his co-workers seem to notice it or care that they're being soaked.
