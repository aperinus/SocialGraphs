Ridley]]. The film tells the story of Hendrix's career beginnings, through his arrival in London, the creation of [[The Jimi Hendrix Experience]] and the beginning of his fame prior to his performance at the [[Monterey Pop Festival]]. It was screened in the Special Presentation section at the [[2013 Toronto International Film Festival]]<ref name=\"TIFF\">{{cite web|url=http://tiff.net/filmsandschedules/festival/2013/allisbymyside |title=All Is by My Side |access-date=5 August 2013 |work=TIFF |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130814011949/http://tiff.net/filmsandschedules/festival/2013/allisbymyside |archive-date=14 August 2013}}</ref> and at the [[South by Southwest|South by Southwest film festival]] and was released in the UK on 8 August 2014.
