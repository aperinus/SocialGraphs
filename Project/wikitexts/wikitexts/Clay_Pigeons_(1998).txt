The film stars [[Joaquin Phoenix]], [[Vince Vaughn]], and [[Janeane Garofalo]].\n\nIt is the second film on-screen collaboration between Vaughn and Phoenix, the first film being ''[[Return to Paradise (1998 film)|Return to Paradise]]'', released  in the same year.\n\n==Plot==\nClay Bidwell, a young man living in Montana, witnesses his friend Earl kill himself because of the ongoing affair that Clay was having with the Earl's adulterous wife Amanda. Feeling guilty, Clay now resists Amanda's advances when she presses him to continue with their affair as if nothing has happened.\n\nAfter a jealous Amanda kills a waitress Clay briefly dates, he inadvertently befriends Lester Long, who happens to be a serial killer. Lester murders the nagging Amanda in an attempt to \"help\" his \"fishing buddy\", leaving Clay horrified. Soon enough, [[Federal Bureau of Investigation|FBI]] agent Dale Shelby and her partner Reynard come to town and zero in on Clay as the prime suspect.\n\n==Cast==\n* [[Joaquin Phoenix]] as Clay Bidwell\n* [[Vince Vaughn]] as Lester Long\n* [[Janeane Garofalo]] as Dale Shelby\n* [[Georgina Cates]] as Amanda\n* [[Gregory Sporleder]] as Earl\n* [[Phil Morris (actor)|Phil Morris]] as Agent Reynard\n* [[Scott Wilson (actor)|Scott Wilson]] as Sheriff Dan Mooney\n* [[Vince Vieluf]] as Deputy Barney\n* Nikki Arlyn as Gloria Collins\n* [[Joseph D.
