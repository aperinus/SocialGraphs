It is a [[Film remake|remake]] of the French film ''[[Bob le flambeur]]'' (1955) by [[Jean-Pierre Melville]]. The film, shot in both [[Monaco]] and [[Nice, France]], follows a [[heroin]]-addicted retired thief through the setup and completion of one last job.\n\n==Cast==\n{{div col|colwidth=30em}}\n* [[Nick Nolte]] as Bob Montagnet\n* [[Emir Kusturica]] as Vladimir\n* [[Nutsa Kukhianidze]] as Anne\n* [[Tch\u00e9ky Karyo]] as Roger\n* [[Sa\u00efd Taghmaoui]] as Paulo\n* Patricia Kell as Yvonne\n* [[G\u00e9rard Darmon]] as Raoul\n* Julien Maurel as Philippe\n* Sarah Bridges as Philipa\n* [[Ralph Fiennes]] as Tony Angel (''uncredited'') \n{{div col end}}\n\n==Reception==\nThe film received mostly positive reviews.
