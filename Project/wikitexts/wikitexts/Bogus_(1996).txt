His mother dies suddenly in a car accident and Albert, who is now an orphan, is sent to New Jersey to live with his mother's foster sister, Harriet ([[Whoopi Goldberg]]). The plot is about Albert, and his imaginary friend named Bogus ([[G\u00e9rard Depardieu]]), a French magician, who helps the boy cope with his transition.
