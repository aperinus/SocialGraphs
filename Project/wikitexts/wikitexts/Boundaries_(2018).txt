It stars [[Vera Farmiga]], [[Christopher Plummer]], [[Lewis MacDougall]], [[Bobby Cannavale]], [[Kristen Schaal]], [[Christopher Lloyd]], and [[Peter Fonda]].\n\nThe film was produced by Automatik Entertainment. It premiered at [[South by Southwest]] on March 12, 2018 and was released in the United States on June 22, 2018, and in Canada on July 6, 2018.
