Marie's husband Niels is a doctor at the hospital, and he falls for Joachim's fiancee Cecilie, and they have an affair. The soundtrack features nine songs written and produced by Jesper Winge Leisner and [[Niels Brinck]], three of which co-written by Anggun.\n\nThe album received positive reception from [[music criticism|music critics]].
