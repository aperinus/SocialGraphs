The film serves as a visual companion to the 2016 album [[Lemonade (Beyonc\u00e9 album)|of the same name]]. It premiered on [[HBO]] on April 23, 2016.\n\n== Premise ==\nThe film is divided into eleven chapters, titled \"Intuition\", \"Denial\", \"Anger\", \"Apathy\", \"Emptiness\", \"Accountability\", Beyonc\u00e9's relationship with her husband, ''Lemonade'' also chronicles the relationship between Black women and American society.
