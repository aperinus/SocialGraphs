The film stars [[Pollyanna McIntosh]], [[Angela Bettis]], [[Sean Bridgers]], [[Lauren Ashley Carter]], Carlee Baker, and Alexa Marcigliano, and introduces Zach Rand and Shyla Molhusen.\n\nThe film was released as part of the [[Bloody Disgusting|Bloody Disgusting Selects]] line.\n\nIn 2019, McIntosh wrote and directed a sequel titled ''[[Darlin' (2019 film)|Darlin']]''.\n\n==Plot==\nAfter the events in [[Offspring (2009 film)|Maine]], an unidentified [[Feral child|feral]] woman is the last remaining member of a cannibalistic tribe that has roamed the Northeast coast for decades. {{Efn|As seen in the 2009 film ''[[Offspring (2009 film)|Offspring]]''.}}\n\nChris Cleek is a county [[lawyer]], who lives with his wife Belle, and their two daughters and son, Peggy, Darlin', and Brian. While out hunting, Chris happens upon the woman, who is bathing and fishing on a creek. The next day, he returns with a net in an effort to capture her. He knocks her out and returns home with her, restrains her in a cellar, and directs his family to participate in \"civilizing\" her. Over the following days, the Cleek family is revealed to be very [[Dysfunctional family|dysfunctional]]. Chris's first attempt to approach the woman results in her biting off and eating his ring finger. Chris orchestrates a violent series of civilizing measures toward the woman.\n\nChris bathes the woman with boiling water and a high-pressure [[power washer]], causing her extreme pain, and is afterward clothed in a dress Belle sewed. Later that night, Chris [[rape]]s the woman while Brian secretly observes through a hole. The next day, Brian also violates the woman with a pair of [[pliers]] but is caught by Peggy.\n\nAfter a series of verbal and physical abuse conducted by both Chris and Brian towards the woman and the family, Belle announces her intention to leave Chris with Peggy and Darlin. In response, Chris knocks Belle unconscious.\n\nPeggy's [[geometry]] teacher Ms. Genevieve Raton, visits the Cleek residence with the suspicion that Chris has been raping Peggy and possibly gotten her [[pregnant]]. Ms. Raton confronts Chris and in response, he knocks her unconscious as well. Chris and Brian tie her up and take her to the barn where he keeps the family dogs. Peggy tries to intervene but becomes a victim of Chris' vicious verbal assault. In the barn, Ms. Raton is attacked by [[Anophthalmia|eyeless]] girl named \"Socket\", the Cleek's secret third daughter who [[Feral child|behaves like the dogs]]. Socket and the dogs kill and [[Eating|consume]] Ms. Raton, as Chris and Brian watch.\n\nPeggy releases the imprisoned woman from the cellar. Belle, having regained consciousness, is attacked and killed by the woman. The woman then proceeds to kill Brian with a saw. Chris tries to shoot the woman but is overpowered, then the woman tears his heart out, and eats it. Afterward, the woman leaves with Darlin and Socket with Peggy following at a distance.\n\n==Cast==\n* [[Pollyanna McIntosh]] as the Woman, the survivor of a tribe of cannibals.\n* [[Sean Bridgers]] as Chris Cleek, a country lawyer.\n* [[Angela Bettis]] as Belle Cleek, the wife of Chris Cleek.\n* [[Lauren Ashley Carter]] as Peggy Cleek, the oldest daughter of Chris Cleek and Belle Cleek.\n* Carlee Baker as Ms. Raton, the teacher of Peggy Cleek.\n* Alexa Marcigliano as Socket Cleek, an eyeless girl, daughter of Chris Cleek and Belle Cleek.\n* Zach Rand as Brian Cleek, the sadistic son of Chris Cleek and Belle Cleek.\n* Shyla Molhusen as Darlin' Cleek, the youngest daughter of Chris Cleek and Belle Cleek.\n\n==Reception==\n{{expand section|date=October 2015}}\nCritical reception for ''The Woman'' has been positive. {{As of|March 2021}}, the film holds a 73% approval rating on [[Rotten Tomatoes]], based on 51 reviews with an average rating of 6.40 out of 10.
