The film stars [[Angela Bassett]], [[Taye Diggs]] (in his film debut), [[Whoopi Goldberg]], and [[Regina King]]. The [[Film score|original music score]] was composed by [[Michel Colombier]].<ref>{{cite news|title= Angela Bassett Reaches a Stellar Groove at Last|author=Gene Seymour|work= The Los Angeles Times|date=August 21, 1998|url= http://articles.latimes.com/1998/aug/21/entertainment/ca-15057|access-date=2010-12-22}}</ref>\n\n==Plot==\n{{expand section|date=September 2020}}\nStella Payne is a very successful 40-year-old stockbroker raising her son, Quincy, and living in [[Marin County, California]], who is persuaded by her best friend from college, Delilah Abraham, to take a well-deserved, first-class vacation to [[Montego Bay, Jamaica]]. As she soaks in the beauty of the island, she encounters a handsome young islander, Winston Shakespeare, who is twenty years her junior. His pursuit of her turns into a blossoming romance that forces Stella to take personal inventory of her life and try to find a balance between her desire for love and companionship, and her responsibilities as a mother and corporate executive.\n\n==Cast==\n* [[Angela Bassett]] as Stella Payne\n* [[Taye Diggs]] as Winston Shakespeare\n* [[Whoopi Goldberg]] as Delilah Abraham\n* [[Regina King]] as Vanessa\n* [[Suzzanne Douglas]] as Angela\n* [[Michael J.
