It also included characters from ''[[13, Rue del Percebe]]'', another comic by the same creator. The film was directed by [[Javier Fesser]] and stars [[Benito Pocino]] and [[Pepe Viyuela]]. It became the second highest-grossing Spanish film of all time.\n\n==Plot==\nThe adventure begins when a spy breaks into Professor Bacterio's lab and steals some of his inventions from the T.I.A. headquarters (''T\u00e9cnicos de Investigaci\u00f3n Aeroterr\u00e1quea'', \"T\u00eda\" being the Spanish word for \"Aunt\", thus a spoof of the [[CIA]]). Mortadelo and Filem\u00f3n who work for the T.I.A. are put on the case, but Fredy, another agent, decides to beat them to it. One of the inventions is a [[weather control]] machine which the spy decides to sell to the President of Tirania, a small [[Eastern Europe]]an [[dictatorship]]. There are numerous clashes between the spy, Fredy, and Mortadelo and Filem\u00f3n (who Fredy seems to want to kill). Our heroes end up in prison after accidentally beating up the local cop once too often. They escape, Filemon's mother gets kidnapped by Fredy and taken to Tirania. The spy is killed in Fredy's place as Fredy ingratiates himself with the President, while having Filem\u00f3n's mother tortured. The heroes arrive and there is a big battle where Filem\u00f3n is apparently killed. Then Mortadelo receives a mortal wound, but Filem\u00f3n gives him a cup of water, which turns out to be the [[Holy Grail]], Mortadelo drinks it and heals quickly, while Fredy gets his [[just deserts]]. Mortadelo and Filem\u00f3n return home, their mission successfully accomplished.\n\n==Cast==\n* [[Benito Pocino]] as Mortadelo\n* [[Pepe Viyuela]] as Filem\u00f3n\n* [[Dominique Pinon]] as Fredy\n*
