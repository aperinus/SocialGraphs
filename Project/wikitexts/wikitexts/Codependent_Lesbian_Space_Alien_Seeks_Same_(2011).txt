space aliens come to Earth, and one of the aliens, Zoinx, falls in love with an employee at a greeting card store named Jane. Jane shyly returns Zoinx's affection and they begin a romance, though Jane does not know that Zoinx is a space alien. In the meantime, Jane, Zoinx, and Zoinx's friends do not know that they may be in trouble: there are two government agents\u2014[[men in black]]\u2014monitoring Jane as she starts to become closer to a woman who the men feel does not belong on this planet.\n\n==Cast==\n{{Cast listing|\n* Lisa Haas as Jane\n* Susan Ziegler as Zoinx\n* Jackie Monahan as Zylar\n* Cynthia Kaplan as Barr\n* Dennis Davis as Senior Agent\n* [[Alex Karpovsky]] as Rookie Agent\n* Rae C.
