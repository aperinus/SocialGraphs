The film contains 26 [[Short film|short]]s, each by different directors spanning fifteen countries, including [[Nacho Vigalondo]], [[Kaare Andrews]], [[Adam Wingard]], [[Simon Barrett (filmmaker)|Simon Barrett]], [[Banjong Pisanthanakun]], [[Ben Wheatley]], [[Lee Hardcastle]], [[Noboru Iguchi]], [[Ti West]], and [[Angela Bettis]].\n\nIt premiered at the [[Toronto International Film Festival]] in 2012.
