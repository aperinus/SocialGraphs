She is desperate to appear as a woman and not a girl, so in order to impress a local boy Ben ([[Dalton James]]), she makes up more and more ridiculous stories, starting with Andr\u00e9 being her lover and leading to some bizarre assumptions by the rest of the community.\n\nAndr\u00e9 is desperate to make Nicole happy (especially as she is increasingly upset by his relationship with girlfriend Isobel) and so plays along with her crazy games, and the stories they make up get increasingly bizarre.\n\n== Cast ==\n*[[G\u00e9rard Depardieu]] as Andr\u00e9 Arnel, Nicole's father\n*[[Katherine Heigl]] as Nicole Arnel, Andr\u00e9's daughter\n*[[Dalton James]] as Ben\n*[[Lauren Hutton]] as Megan Arnel, Andr\u00e9's ex-wife and Nicole's mother\n*[[Faith Prince]] as Diana\n*[[Stephen Tobolowsky]] as Mike\n*[[Ann Hearn]] as Stella\n*[[Robyn Peterson]] as Doris\n*[[Frank Renzulli]] as Fred\n*[[Manny Jacobs]] as Raymond\n*[[Baha Men|Jeffrey Chea]] as Pablo\n*[[Stephen Burrows (actor)|Stephen Burrows]] as Hakim\n*[[Michael Robinson (actor)|Michael Robinson]] as Tom\n*[[Robert Miner]] as Mr. Porter\n*[[Betty Miner]] as Mrs. Porter\n*[[Emma Thompson]] as Isobel, Andr\u00e9's girlfriend (''uncredited'')\n*[[Roberto Escobar]] as Alberto\n\n==Filming==\nFilming occurred during the summer of 1993.
