The film is structured in three anthological episodes, presented as the chapters of Moretti's open diary, in which he describes his thoughts about various [[slice of life]] situations.\n\nThe film was selected to compete for the [[Palme d'Or]] at the [[1994 Cannes Film Festival]], where Moretti won the [[Best Director Award (Cannes Film Festival)|Best Director Award]].<ref name=\"festival-cannes.com\"/>\n\n==Plot==\n=== Chapter I: ''In Vespa'' (''On My Vespa'') ===\nPutting the hot [[Rome|Roman]] summer to good use,\u00a0[[Nanni Moretti]]\u00a0dedicates himself to his favorite hobby, riding his\u00a0[[Vespa]]\u00a0through the streets of the half-deserted\u00a0city.\n \nHere, Moretti lets the landscape inspire his thoughts: he laments the banalization of politics in contemporary\u00a0[[Italian cinema]], comments on the [[gentrification]] of the [[quarters of Rome]], mocks the overzealous critical reception of movies like\u00a0''[[Henry: Portrait of a Serial Killer]]'', and confesses his love for dance caused by the movie ''[[Flashdance]]'' \u2013later meeting its\u00a0star [[Jennifer Beals]]. Finally, he visits the place in [[Ostia (Rome)|Ostia]] where\u00a0[[Pier Paolo Pasolini]]\u00a0was killed, to pay his respects.\n\n=== Chapter II: ''Isole'' (''Islands'') ===\nHaving to work on an idea for a new movie, Moretti decides to leave Rome for a while for the more peaceful [[Aeolian Islands]]. In [[Lipari Islands|Lipari]] he's the guest of his friend Gerardo, an austere scholar who moved there eleven years earlier to better study [[James Joyce]]'s ''[[Ulysses (novel)|Ulysses]]'' without being distracted by modern commodities such as television, which he despises. However, both are unable to find the tranquility they're searching for since the number of tourists has suddenly increased.\n\nThey move to [[Salina Island|Salina]], where they are guests of two couples friends of Gerardo, both incapable of managing their hyperactive children, a feature that seems rampant on the entire island. Meanwhile, Gerardo begins watching television and gradually becomes completely addicted to it, and especially to [[soap opera]]s. They move again, this time to [[Stromboli Island|Stromboli]], where they are constantly bothered by a megalomaniac mayor who tries to involve them in the oddest projects.\n\nThey finally move to\u00a0[[Alicudi]], frugal and isolated, lacking water and electricity. Here Moretti seems to have found the right place to focus on his project, but soon Gerardo runs away in despair to catch the last [[ferry]], unable to live without his favorite soap operas, while disavowing his ideals and proclaiming undying love for cheap entertainment.\n\n=== Chapter III: ''Medici'' (''Doctors'') ===\nMoretti\u00a0[[Breaking the fourth wall|clarifies to the audience]]\u00a0that the following chapter will be based on a true story happened to him some years earlier; one day, he begins to suffer from persistent [[itching]] and [[insomnia]]. He visits many doctors and specialists, but they all dismiss him with different diagnosis, prescribing to him a lot of costly drugs and prohibiting him to eat most of his favorite food. Seeing no improvements, Moretti unsuccessfully tries [[Alternative medicine|alternative cures]] like [[reflexology]] and [[acupuncture]].\n\nAfter almost a year, a doctor notices his developing cough during a visit and suggests him an [[X-ray]]. That reveals a mass on his lung, which after a\u00a0[[biopsy]]\u00a0is discovered to be a\u00a0still-curable [[Hodgkin's lymphoma]]. Moretti successfully goes through [[chemotherapy]] and has the lymphoma cured. Sometime later, he reads the definition of Hodgkin's lymphoma in a basic [[medical encyclopedia]], finding out that its most common symptoms are exactly itching\u00a0and\u00a0insomnia. Surrounded by the dozens of useless drugs he bought, Moretti laments the incapability of most doctors of listening to their patients, before making a bitter [[Toast (honor)|toast]] \"to health\" with a glass of water.\n\n== Cast ==\nSource:<ref name=\"Lancia\"/>\n; Chapter I (''In Vespa'')\n{{Castlist|\n* [[Nanni Moretti]] as himself\n* [[Giulio Base]] as car driver\n* Giovanna Bozzolo as actor in
