While there, he is presumed to have been killed in a building collapse. His wife travels to the region to find him, believing him to be in the city of [[Vukovar]]. Travelling through the war-torn landscape, she arrives in the city, and bears witness to the [[Vukovar massacre|massacre which took place there]].
