2011|access-date=23 September 2011}}</ref>\n\n==Synopsis==\n{{Anchor|Plot|Synopsis}}\nThe film chronicles the journey of puppeteer [[Kevin Clash]], the unlikely voice and mastermind behind [[Elmo]]. Focusing on Clash's childhood dreams and early career in [[Baltimore, Maryland]]; it covers his first meeting and relationship with [[Sesame Street]] creator [[Jim Henson]], puppet maker [[Kermit Love]], and takes you on a journey that is the phenomenal success of [[Elmo]].<ref>{{cite web|title='Being Elmo': Covering Raw Talent With Bright Red Fur|url=https://www.npr.org/2011/10/20/137340547/being-elmo-covering-raw-talent-with-bright-red-fur|website=NPR|date=October 20, 2011|access-date=20 October 2011|last1=Holmes|first1=Linda}}</ref>\n\n''[[Sesame Street]]'' camera man [[James Miller (cinematographer)|James Miller]] originally filmed [[Kevin Clash]] with [[Elmo]] looking at photos of Miller's 2 year-old daughter. After showing the footage to Constance Marks, she started thinking maybe this would be a good documentary, seeing the performer behind the [[muppet]].
