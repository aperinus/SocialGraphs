Earlier, a hiker had found an ancient book of spells and after reading a few lines had turned into a demon.  Hearing noise in the brush, the group thinks they have found a deer, only to discover it is the transformed hiker.  Fearful, the groups shoots at the demon and takes refuge in a nearby cabin. As more and more zombie-fied creatures attack the group, they use every implement available to defend themselves.
