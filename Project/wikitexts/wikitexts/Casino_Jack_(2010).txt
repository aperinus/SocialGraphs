[[comedy-drama]] [[thriller film]] directed by [[George Hickenlooper]] and starring [[Kevin Spacey]]. The film focuses on the career of [[Washington, D.C.]] lobbyist and businessman [[Jack Abramoff]], who was involved in a massive [[Jack Abramoff Indian lobbying scandal|corruption scandal]] that led to his conviction as well as the conviction of two [[White House]] officials, [[United States House of Representatives|Rep.]] [[Bob Ney]], and nine other lobbyists and congressional staffers.
