The film stars [[India Eisley]], [[Callan McAuliffe]] and [[Samuel L. Jackson]].\n\n==Synopsis==\nSociety has degenerated into a slum where people have taken to kidnapping children and selling them to flesh cartels. A young woman named Sawa (India Eisley) has been pretending to be a prostitute in order to kill some of the cartel leaders, ultimately trying to reach the main leader, Emir, who had killed her parents. After a kill, she finds she needs to relax by taking a drug called \"Amp\", which also [[Mindwipe|wipes her memories]].\n\n==Cast==\n* [[India Eisley]] as Sawa\n* [[Callan McAuliffe]] as Oburi\n* [[Samuel L.
