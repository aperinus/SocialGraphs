It currently has an approval rating of 90% on review aggregator website [[Rotten Tomatoes]], based on 70 reviews, with an average rating of 7.8/10. The website's critical consensus reads, \"So you wanna be a rock 'n' roll star? ''Dig!'' compellingly chronicles the ups and downs of the Dandy Warhols and the Brian Jonestown Massacre, two ambitious bands whose love/hate relationship embodies many of the potential pratfalls of the music business.\"<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/dig/ |title=Dig!
