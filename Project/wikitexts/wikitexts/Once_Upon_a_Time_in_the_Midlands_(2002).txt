It is set in [[Nottingham]], in the [[East Midlands]] region of England.\n\n==Plot==\nSet in Nottinghamshire, Dek ([[Rhys Ifans]]) proposes to his girlfriend Shirley ([[Shirley Henderson]]) on TV.  When Jimmy ([[Robert Carlyle]]), \"the great love of her life\" and father of her daughter Marlene ([[Finn Atkins]]), sees this, he returns in an attempt to win back her heart. However, after deserting his friends in [[Scotland]] during an unsuccessful [[robbery]] of some clowns, his friends turn against him and come to the Midlands to try to track him down.
