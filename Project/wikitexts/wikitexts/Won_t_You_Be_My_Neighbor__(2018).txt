The trailer for the film debuted on what would have been Rogers's 90th birthday, March 20, 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://deadline.com/2018/03/wont-you-be-my-neighbor-trailer-mister-rogers-morgan-neville-documentary-1202348337/|title='Won't You Be My Neighbor?' Trailer: Mister Rogers Reveals Guiding Force|first=Greg|last=Evans|date=March 20, 2018|website=[[Deadline Hollywood]]|publisher=[[Penske Business Media]]|access-date=March 26, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://abcnews.go.com/GMA/Culture/wont-neighbor-trailer-reveals-radical-mister-rogers/story?id=53906327|title='Won't You Be My Neighbor' trailer reveals 'radical' Mister Rogers|first=Michael|last=Rothman|date=March 21, 2018|website=[[Good Morning America]]|publisher=[[ABC News]]|access-date=March 26, 2018}}</ref>\n\nThe film premiered at the [[2018 Sundance Film Festival]] and was released in the United States on June 8, 2018. It received acclaim from critics and audiences and grossed over $22 million, becoming the top-grossing biographical documentary ever produced and the 12th largest-grossing documentary ever produced.<ref name=\"gibson\">{{cite news |last1=Gibson |first1=Caitlin |title=How Mister Rogers became a timeless oracle of parenting wisdom |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/lifestyle/on-parenting/how-mister-rogers-became-a-timeless-oracle-of-parenting-wisdom/2019/08/10/810f79dc-b488-11e9-951e-de024209545d_story.html?wpisrc=nl_optimist&wpmm=1 |access-date=August 18, 2019 |work=Washington Post |date=August 12, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=McNary |first1=Dave |title='Won't You Be My Neighbor', 'RBG' Nab Producers Guild Documentary Nominations |url=https://variety.com/2018/film/news/producers-guild-documentary-nominations-2019-1203033482/ |access-date=August 18, 2019 |work=Variety |date=November 20, 2019}}</ref>\n\nIt was nominated for numerous awards, won the [[Independent Spirit Award for Best Documentary Feature]], and was chosen by ''[[Time (magazine)|Time]]'' magazine as one of its top ten films of 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://time.com/5454739/best-movies-2018/|title=The Top 10 Movies of 2018|last=Zacharek|first=Stephanie|author-link=Stephanie Zacharek|date=November 15, 2018|work=[[Time (magazine)|Time]]|access-date=November 27, 2018}}</ref>\n\n==Premise==\n\nFilmmaker Morgan Neville examines the life and legacy of Fred Rogers, the beloved host of the popular children's television program ''[[Mister Rogers' Neighborhood]]''.\n\n==Production==\nOne of the film's producers was Nicholas Ma, son of the famed cellist [[Yo-Yo Ma]]. As a child, the younger Ma appeared twice as himself in ''Mister Rogers' Neighborhood''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://cranberries.medium.com/nicholas-ma-tells-the-story-of-fred-rogers-in-wont-you-be-my-neighbor-829b81dbf33b|title = Nicholas Ma Tells the Story of Fred Rogers in \"Won't You be My Neighbor?\"|date = June 7, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.thespectrum.com/story/news/local/2018/08/25/producer-nicholas-ma-insights-fred-rogers-documentary/1097232002/|title = 'He would give you comfort but he wouldn't let you escape': Producer discusses Fred Rogers}}</ref>\n\n==Appearances==\n\n* Joanne Rogers (Fred's widow)\n* John Rogers (Fred's son)\n* Jim Rogers (Fred's son)\n* Elaine Rogers Crozier (Fred's sister)\n* Margy Whitmer (Fred's producer from 1979 onward)\n* Hedda Sharapan\n* [[Fran\u00e7ois Clemmons|Fran\u00e7ois Scarborough Clemmons]]\n* [[Tom Junod]]\n* [[Yo-Yo Ma]]\n* [[Joe Negri]]\n* [[David Newell]] (Mr. McFeely)\n* Howard and Pam Erlanger (parents of [[Jeff Erlanger]])\n* [[Jeff Erlanger]] in archival recordings\n* [[Fred Rogers]] in archival recordings\n* [[Koko (gorilla)|Koko]] in archival recordings\n* [[Hilary Hahn]]\n\n==Release==\n\n''Won't You Be My Neighbor?'' was shown at many film festivals including on January 19, 2018, at the [[Sundance Film Festival]], February 24, 2018, at the [[Boulder International Film Festival]], March 2, 2018, at the [[True/False Film Festival]], March 16, 2018, at the [[Miami International Film Festival|Miami Film Festival]], and May 5, 2018, at the [[Montclair Film|Montclair Film Festival]]. It had a limited release in US theaters on June 15, 2018.<ref>{{cite journal |last= Mendelson |first= Scott |title= Friday Box Office: 'Ocean's 8' Drops 62%, 'Hereditary' and 'Solo' Hold |date= June 16, 2018 |access-date= June 17, 2018|journal= [[Forbes]] |url= https://www.forbes.com/sites/scottmendelson/2018/06/16/friday-box-office-hereditary-oceans8-solo-star-wars-incredibles-deadpool-upgrade}}</ref> [[PBS]] aired the film on February 9, 2019, as part of the network's ''[[Independent Lens]]'' series.<ref>{{cite news |title=Won't You Be My Neighbor?
