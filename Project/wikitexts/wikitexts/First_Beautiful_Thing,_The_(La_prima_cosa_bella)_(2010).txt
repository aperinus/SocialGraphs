The film stars Micaela Ramazzotti, Valerio Mastandrea, Claudia Pandolfi and Stefania Sandrelli. On 9 November 2010 the film opened at the Cinema Italian style Film Festival in Los Angeles.\n\n==Plot==\nThe film tells the story of the Michelucci family, from the 1970s to the present day: the central character is the stunningly beautiful Anna, the lively, frivolous and sometimes embarrassing mother of Bruno and Valeria. Everything begins in the Summer of 1971, at the annual Summer beauty pageant held at Livorno\u2019s most popular bathing establishment. Anna is unexpectedly crowned \u201cMost Beautiful Mother\u201d, unwittingly stirring the violent jealousy of her husband, a [[Non-commissioned officer]] of [[Carabinieri]]. From then on, chaos strikes the family and for Anna, Bruno and his sister Valeria, it is the start of an adventure that will only end thirty years later. The whole movie is set when Bruno, a high school teacher who ended up living in [[Milan]] after managing to escape from Livorno and his mother, returns to his hometown to be at his mother\u2019s side during her very last days, remembering many of the key episodes of his family life through a long series of [[flashback (narrative)|flashback]]s.\n\n==Cast==\n* [[Valerio Mastandrea]] as Bruno Michelucci\n* [[Micaela Ramazzotti]] as Anna Nigiotti in Michelucci - 1971-1981\n* [[Stefania Sandrelli]] as Anna Nigiotti in Michelucci - 2008\n* [[Claudia Pandolfi]] as Valeria Michelucci\n* [[Marco Messeri]] as Il Nesi\n* [[Fabrizia Sacchi]] as Sandra\n* [[Aurora Frasca]] as Valeria Michelucci - child\n* [[Giacomo Bibbiani]] as Bruno Michelucci - child\n* [[Giulia Burgalassi]] as Valeria Michelucci - teenager\n* [[Francesco Rapalino]] as Bruno - teenager\n* [[Sergio Albelli]] as Mario Michelucci\n* [[Isabella Cecchi]] as Zia Leda Nigiotti\n* [[Emanuele Barresi]] as Roberto Lenzi\n* [[Dario Ballantini]] as Avvocato Cenerini\n* [[Paolo Ruffini (actor)|Paolo Ruffini]] as Cristiano Cenerini\n\n==Awards and nominations==\nThe film was nominated for 18 [[David di Donatello]] Awards, and won Best Screenplay ([[Paolo Virz\u00ec]], [[Francesco Bruni (screenwriter)|Francesco Bruni]], [[Francesco Piccolo]]), Best Actress [[Micaela Ramazzotti]] Best Actor [[Valerio Mastandrea]]. 4 [[Silver Ribbon]], Director of the best film [[Paolo Virz\u00ec]], Best screenplay, Best Actress [[Stefania Sandrelli]] and [[Micaela Ramazzotti]], Best Costume [[Gabriella Pescucci]].
